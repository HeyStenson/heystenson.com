HEY STENSON

I'm Heather Stenson! I'm a full-stack developer, a voracious learner, and Ruby nerd. I'm often found building things, reading books, running runs, and/or petting cats.

I am a librarian turned full-stack developer - and those two fields have a lot more in common that most people think! I'm passionate about creating ways of connecting people with information - and connecting people with other people - using current technologies.

What are we going to build together?

Moment

Moment is a micro-diary app that allows users to create journals and journal entries and upload their own images from their computer, Facebook, or Instagram.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, jQuery, Devise

moodhue

moodhue allows users to create a beautiful visual representation of their daily moods.

Technologies: jQuery, Node.js, Express, Moment.js, MongoDB

Encore

This app connects clients seeking entertainers for their events to performers. Users sign up in order to contact performers, and can additionally create performer profiles for themselves.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap
